An Interactive Map of Literary Boston: 1794–1862

This map provides specific locations associated with Boston-based writers, editors, publishers, and periodicals during the years covered by the Forgotten Chapters exhibition. In the pop-up descriptions that feature 2 addresses, the first is current, while the second refers to the same location as it was known in the years specified. Because many streets, street names, and building numbers have changed, some current addresses are approximate.
